Module DBEnv

module DBEnv: sig .. end

De Bruijn environments



An environment is used to map De Bruijn indices to objects (or nothing).

This is low level and should be used with a lot of care (it's mostly used in InnerTerm.DB and a lot of coffee or aspiring at hand.
